What We Do

WHAT WE DO

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to helping third-world
countries fight poverty. We provide humanitarian aid by way of denim
jeans that provide income to impoverished individuals and families. We
raise awareness by inspiring others to help in an effort to improve the
economic conditions of their world. Back in 1998, while living in Europe,
Sheyna Axtell, the founder, was introduced to intolerable living
conditions. She came up with a way to provide long-term employment
to those who's opportunities were otherwise limited.
